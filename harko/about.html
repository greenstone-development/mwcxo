About Harko

Refined Excellence

Meet Our President
and Founder

Our President and Founder, Brad Teets, has been building custom luxury homes in Denver for more than 18 years. He began his construction career building custom homes with his father's building company Carter Custom Homes at the young age of 14. Through this experience, he gained a deep understanding of electrical work, framing, interior trim and tile installation and the importance of putting client needs and desires above all else — no exceptions.

After a positive and rewarding career working with his father, Brad branched off from the family building company to tackle the market creating his own mark and legacy. Brad's most notable competitive advantage within the industry is his well established relationships with his trades. He has known more than a few of his accountable tradesmen for up to 20 years.
